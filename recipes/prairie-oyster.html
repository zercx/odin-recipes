Prairie Oyster Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 tsp Malt Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Horseradish
  • 1 Dash Salt
  • 1 Dash Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Pour liquor of your choice into the glass
  2. Crack and separate an egg yolk into a lowball glass.
  3. Add malt vinegar, worcestershire sauce, horseradish.
  4. Add a dash of salt and black pepper.
  5. Consume the prairie oyster in a single gulp.
